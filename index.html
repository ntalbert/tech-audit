Positioning Opportunities

Exploit competitor vulnerabilities and differentiate Rhombus in the market

Born in the Cloud

vs. 'Transitioning to Cloud'

Avigilon and Milestone acquired their cloud capabilities. Rhombus built cloud-native from inception.

Key Message: Emphasize simplicity, no legacy complexity, faster deployment. Verkada is the only other true cloud-native player.

Trust & Security Leadership

vs. Verkada's Breaches

Verkada's 2021 breach (150K feeds exposed) and 2024 FTC settlement ($2.95M) create opening.

Key Message: Emphasize cybersecurity founding team, SOC 2/NDAA/GDPR compliance, security-first architecture.

Open Platform

vs. Closed Ecosystems

Rhombus's 100% open API and 40+ integrations counter Verkada's more closed ecosystem.

Key Message: Rhombus Relay (cloud-managing existing cameras) addresses 'no rip and replace' buyer concern.

Mid-Market Sweet Spot

vs. Enterprise & Startup Focus

Enterprise buyers prefer Avigilon/Milestone credibility. Startups default to Verkada brand.

Key Message: Position as ideal for growth-stage organizations wanting enterprise capability without complexity.

"The window is favorable: cloud video security grows at 15-18% CAGR, AI capabilities are table stakes, and Verkada's trust challenges create positioning opportunity."

Speed of execution will determine whether Rhombus consolidates as the clear #2 challenger.
